2 ## Resistance's Portable-Adventure-Game-Engine

4 ![](docs/img/rpage_logo.png)

6 **R-PAGE** (pronounced 'arp√®ge') is a **C framework** meant to accelerate the creation of **adventure games** for the Amiga OCS/ECS range of computers. It is designed with portability in mind, speed performance being a lesser concern.<br>
7 R-PAGE is brought to you by the **demoscene** group [RESISTANCE](https://resistance.no/).
8 
9 * Written in C language
10 * AmigaOS & OS-friendly for all the access to the Amiga hardware
11 * Portable code, builds on SAS/C
12 * Reportedly works on MorphOS using the built-in AmigaOS emulation layer
13 * AMIGA OCS/ECS only (AGA is on the roadmap)

19 ### What can I do with this ?
20 At the moment, R-PAGE is more a framework than a full feature engine. Feel free to use it as a boostrap for your own adventure project. The roadmap, however, includes a very basic adventure game to showcase the features of this framework.
